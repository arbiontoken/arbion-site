How to Buy ARBN Safely

Avoid fake tokens and minimize mistakes when swapping on-chain.

Always verify the contract

The only official ARBN contract address:

0x16C7eE66633586f781fc47960221DC1bF108906F

Verify on BscScan ↗

Step-by-step

  1. Install a wallet (e.g., MetaMask) and switch to BNB Chain.
  2. Have a small amount of BNB for gas.
  3. Open PancakeSwap swap page.
  4. Paste the contract address into the token selector (don’t search by name).
  5. Start with a small test swap first.
Open swap with ARBN pre-filled ↗

Common mistakes

  • Wrong network: you must be on BNB Chain mainnet, not testnet.
  • Fake token: never trust “ARBN” search results — paste the contract.
  • High slippage: small pools can cause big price swings and failed swaps.
  • Approval scams: don’t sign unknown approvals on random sites.
This is not financial advice. On-chain swaps carry risk (slippage, MEV, volatility).
